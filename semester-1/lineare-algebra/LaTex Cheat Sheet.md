### General
`\not{command}` --> $\not{command}$
	==> `\not=` --> $\not=$

`command^{upper}` --> $command^{upper}$ 
`command_{lower}` --> $command_{lower}$
--> can be nested:
	`c_{low}^{high}` --> $c_{low}^{high}$
	
### Logic
###### not
`\lnot` --> $\lnot$
###### and
`\land` --> $\land$ 
###### or
`\lor` --> $\lor$ 
###### implication/subjunction
`\rightarrow` --> $\rightarrow$    
`\Rightarrow` --> $\Rightarrow$ 
###### equvalence
`\leftrightarrow` --> $\leftrightarrow$  
`\Leftrightarrow` --> $\Leftrightarrow$

###### Quantors
`\forall` --> $\forall$ 




### Mengen
###### Element
`\in` --> $\in$ 
`x \in M` --> $x \in M$
 
###### Teilmenge
`\subset` --> $\subset$ 
`subseteq` --> $\subseteq$

###### Escape Character
`\(character)` --> esacapes special character ( e.g. '{' )

###### Summe 
`\sum` --> $\sum$ 
`\sum_{k = 1}^{n} expression` --> $\sum_{k = 1}^{n} expression$

###### Spezielle Mengen
`\mathbb{N}` --> $\mathbb{N}$

### spacing
