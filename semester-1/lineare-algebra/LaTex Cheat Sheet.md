### General
`\not{command}` --> $\not{command}$
	==> `\not=` --> $\not=$


### Logic
###### not
`\lnot` --> $\lnot$
###### and
`\land` --> $\land$ 
###### or
`\lor` --> $\lor$ 
###### implication/subjunction
`\rightarrow` --> $\rightarrow$    
`\Rightarrow` --> $\Rightarrow$ 
###### equvalence
`\leftrightarrow` --> $\leftrightarrow$  
`\Leftrightarrow` --> $\Leftrightarrow$




### Mengen
###### Element
`\in` --> $\in$ 
`x \in M` --> $x \in M$
 
###### Teilmenge
`\subset` --> $\subset$ 
`subseteq` --> $\subseteq$

###### Escape Character
`\(character)` --> esacapes special character ( e.g. '{' )

###### Summe 
`\sum` --> $\sum$ 
`\sum_{k = 1}^{n} expression` --> $\sum_{k = 1}^{n} expression$

###### Spezielle Mengen
`\mathbb{N}` --> $\mathbb{N}$

### spacing
`\.` --> wee lil space
`\ ` --> regular spac
`\quad` --> big space
`\qquad` --> way too big space
